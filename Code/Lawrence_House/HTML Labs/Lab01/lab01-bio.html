Junji Ito: The best horror manga artist!


Wikipedia: Junji Ito

When anyone thinks of horror manga, there are two great mangaka that come to mind. One of those is none other than Junji Ito. Raised in Nakatsugawa, Gifu Japan, He got his love of horror from reading the magazines his older sisters would buy. Ito began writing and drawing manga in 1984. In 1984, he won an honorable mention after submitting an entry to a monthly magazine, one of the judges being his inspirer, Kazuo Umezu.

"I am a horror maniac who prefers to stay at home." - Junji Ito
