Sly Marbo, sometimes called the "One Man Army", is one of the most decorated members of the Astra Militarum in Imperial history. A revered member of the Catachan Jungle Fighters, he is known to possess unparalleled military skills. Including, but not limited to:

  • Sniper Rifle skills that shame Vindicare Assassins.
  • The grit and determination of a veteran Astares.
  • The predatory instinct of a Tyranid Lictor.
  • Blade skills that humiliate Eldar Banshees.
It is not known whether or not the name "Sly" is an earned name as per Catachan tradition, or his actual given one.

Here are some facts about Sly Marbo:

  • Sly Marbo threw a grenade and killed five Orks. Then it exploded.
  • A Catachan Devil once stung Sly Marbo. After 5 solar days of agonising pain, the Catachan Devil died.
  • Flak Armour wears Sly Marbo for protection.
  • When Ambulls go to bed at night, they check their nests for Sly Marbo.
  • Sly Marbo doesn't shower, he takes blood baths.
  • Sly Marbo sleeps with a pillow under his gun.
  • Sly Marbo does not sleep. He waits.
