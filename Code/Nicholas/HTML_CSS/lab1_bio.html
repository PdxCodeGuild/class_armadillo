Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson


Bio

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is an actor, entrepreneur, former football player and retired professional wrestler. He has starred in several blockbusters flicks including: The Mummy Returns, The Scorpion King, Moana, San Andreas, and the Fast and the Furious franchise. He also starred in all five seasons of the HBO comedy-drama Ballers Johnson's wrestling career spanned 16 years with the World Wrestling Federation and World Wrestling Entertainment where he was coveted as the greater professional wrestler of all time. He is a husband and father of 3, and partakes in political activism and philanthropy. Johnson's multifaceted career has earned him a spot on the 100 Most Influential People in the World list for both 2016 and 2019. Learn more about "The Great One."
