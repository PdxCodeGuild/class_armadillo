Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson



Bio

Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is an actor, entrepreneur, former football player and retired professional wrestler. He has starred in several blockbusters Hollywood motion pictures including: The Mummy Returns, The Scorpion King, Moana, San Andreas, and the Fast and the Furious franchise. He also starred in all five seasons of the HBO comedy-drama Ballers. Johnson's wrestling career spanned 16 years with the World Wrestling Federation and World Wrestling Entertainment where he was coveted as the greatest professional wrestler of all time. He is a husband, a proud father of 3. In his free time he partakes in philanthropy and political activism, and has shown interest in running for President in the future. Johnson's multifaceted career has earned him a spot on the Time 100 Most Influential People in the World list for both 2016 and 2019. Learn more about "The Great One."
